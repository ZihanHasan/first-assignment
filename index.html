Our Donate Plan

We works among the helpless, homeless, children and youths to develop a Kingdom of God minded new generation. We work to establish the Kingdom principles in and through the next generation.

Food for a family

Today, 690 million people around the world will go to bed on an empty stomach. But your gift means that one less will be at risk of starvation tonight. Whether you decide to make a one-off donation or set up a monthly gift, you can change a life forever today.

Shelter for a family

1.7 million people around the world experience homelessness every year. They are living on the streets in major cities, small towns, and in most suburbs. We are working to end the cycle of homelessness. Our programs empower youth and help end the cycle of homelessness.

Education for a child

Orphans do not have the same opportunities as normal children. They are often forced into child labour to earn money. Given the opportunity of going back to school, these orphans could develop practical skills, which would enable their communities to break out of the cycle of poverty.

Volunteering Opportunities

Our volunteers play an important role in helping us to restore hope to the displaced and disadvantaged in our community. When you volunteer with us, it's not just a chance to do good for the community but it's also a great way to cultivate your skills or to carry out a project with your friends.
